## Standard English short wording

A10-STMS is a nondimensional reduced-surrogate audit of mission-variable-preserving hybrid thermal control. It is not spacecraft-ready cooling technology, thermal-vacuum-validated technology, a flight controller, a certified aerospace safety system, or a hardware thermal-control design.

## Standard Japanese short wording
